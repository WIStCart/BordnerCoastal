    Should NETPRPTA (Net Property Tax) include special charges such as delinquent payments?

    NETPRPTA (Net Property Tax) should reflect:

  • Net property-taxes for the single tax year only. Delinquent property taxes from prior years should not be included within this sum.
  • Only net property tax amounts. Any other non-tax related charges, such as delinquent utilities, should not be included.

    • Why are my non-parcel features being flagged as "Appears to be a duplicate value in PARCELID" when they are actually labeled properly according to the PARCELID schema definition (i.e. "HWY ROW" or "LONG LAKE") as non-tax parcels?

    The logic within the duplicate value function of the validation tool checks against a list of non-tax parcels that were derived from the V2 Statewide Parcel Layer. Any PARCELID strings found within this list will not be flagged as duplicates. However, if the string is not found within this list, then it will be flagged. Values not included in the list are still valid as long as they meet the PARCELID schema definition.

    How should Union High School (UHS) districts be populated in SCHOOLDIST and SCHOOLDISTNO?

    According to Wisconsin Department of Public Instruction (DPI), a school district may be categorized in many ways and one of the ways of categorization is by grades operated. The three possible categories for operable grades are:

  • pre - kindergarten to Grade 12 (PK - 12);
  • elementary (PK - 8); and
  • union high school or UHS (9 - 12).

    • *The the elementary (PK - 8) and UHS (9 - 12) categories share the same territory, and multiple elementary districts are geographically nested within for each Union High School District. DPI cites ten total Union High School Districts across the state of Wisconsin (see the list here).

    A tax parcel should not contain multiple school districts. For any areas that apply a Union High School District (UHS), the UHS district should be the only district populating this field,. Elementary districts within a UHS are known as ‘children’ of the ‘parent’ UHS district and should not be included in the data submission.
